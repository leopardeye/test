Main building

The lodge is situated about 300m from the bungalows. The lodge is accessible by car. We have a fully functioning restaurant, serving breakfast, lunch and dinner. There is a bar, infinity pool and lounge to relax in. The lodge has also a luxury unit with an en-suite bathroom which is ideal for older guests.
